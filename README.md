# LeadScout
An autonomous AI Sales Development Representative (SDR) that scrapes, reads, and qualifies B2B leads in real-time using Playwright and Gemini 1.5 Flash.


Detailed Description
LeadScout is a high-performance lead qualification engine designed to solve the "low-quality data" problem faced by Lead Generation agencies. While most scrapers only provide raw contact info, LeadScout uses LLM-integrated automation to perform deep qualitative analysis on every prospect before they ever hit your CRM.

The Problem It Solves:
Lead Gen companies often waste hours manually vetting scraped lists to see if a company is actually a "fit." LeadScout automates this "eye-balling" process, using AI to browse a website just like a human SDR would.

Core Features:
Deep-Link Crawling: Uses Playwright to bypass surface-level homepages and extract context from "About," "Services," and "Case Study" pages.
AI Vetting Engine: Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash to analyze business models, industry niches, and company size against specific client criteria.
Automated Lead Scoring: Assigns a 1-10 "Match Score" based on custom parameters (e.g., "Is this a mid-market financial firm in Gauteng?").
Structured Intelligence: Delivers not just data, but reasoning. Every lead comes with a "Why they are a fit" summary generated by the AI.

Technical Architecture:
Automation: Python & Playwright (Async)
Inference: Google Gemini API (Multimodal/Text)
Storage: JSON/CSV Output ready for CRM import (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
Targeting: Optimized for regional business directories and localized Google Search results.

Ideal For:
Lead Gen Agencies looking to automate their vetting process.
Sales Teams targeting niche B2B markets (like Finance, Tech, or Legal).
SaaS Founders building automated outreach workflows.
